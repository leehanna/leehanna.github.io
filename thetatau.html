Spring '16 Rush

The flyer for this rush was a lego man getting “built” by Theta Tau. I chose Legos for the theme of rush since Legos were a part of most engineers’ childhood. The target of this flyer was broad: I appealed to females with bright colors and a rounded font, but the slogan “Get Built” was targeted towards the male audience.

I went with a simple classic black longsleeve look for the rush shirt.

Fall '16 Rush

As the Fall 2016 Recruitment Chair, my co-chair and I decided on a theme centering around Big Hero 6, a Disney movie centered around a robot friend (technology). The main targeted audience was female college students to improve the diversity of the club. To capture the attention of college students, I thought Big Hero 6 would be perfect because it would help establish an instant emotional connection.
