Summary

I am a recent grad with a passion for all parts of the design stack and a background in computer science and web development. My previous work largely consists of hybrid developer and designer positions where I was a one-woman team. I found UX problems and initiated projects to fix them, iterated on my designs with user feedback and detailed wireframes, and brought my designs to life with code.

Currently based in sunny San Diego

How I got into design

When I was in high school in 2011, I interned as a layout designer at a publishing company, and I was amazed at how much thought and strategy went behind every single element on a page. In college, I studied computer science because I enjoyed how it was like solving brainteasers and I could build cool stuff with code. When I wasn't programming, I was designing marketing materials for my clubs, and I later took two part-time jobs as a hybrid designer and developer. I felt that UI/UX combined all of my interests into one, since I am intrigued by psychology, product strategy, and technology.
