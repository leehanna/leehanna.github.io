A startup in its really early stages that is looking to transform the pet industry by providing an all inclusive app for pet owners.

My Roles

Defining the brand image
Conceptualizing and designing the mobile UIUX framework

The Challenge

The main challenge was to start from a blank slate to create a framework that is flexible as a social media platform to a Yelp-like database of services to a future pet sitter finder.

The Process - Logo

I designed variations with different font pairings, and included a pointy dog and cat hybrid that was aimed to be more inclusive of more animals. After surveying target users, the blue color and mouthless dog was chosen.

The Process - Profile

Since the profile needed to include as much information as compactly as possible without overcrowding the screen, I went through a couple of iterations of the profile user interface. I improved each iteration with feedback from the team and by negotiating with the developers for more screen space allotted to profile information.

The Process - Landing Page and First Time User Experience

I sketched out a couple of wireframes for the first time user experience and landing page. I researched how other websites made their landing pages conducive to discovering new content and allowing users to sign up quickly and easily, and adapted my findings on my wireframes. After receving feedback from the team and some of my friends, I fleshed out the design.

Reflections

Conceptualizing the product from scratch was a very fun but challenging process for me. For an intuitive interface, I researched various social media platforms and combined elements from them for the base framework. My only regret was that I could not stray far from conventional UIs without interviewing and testing on a large target user base.
