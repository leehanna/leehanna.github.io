An early stage startup looking to transform the pet industry by providing an all-inclusive app for pet owners.
Visit the website at spetcial.com.

My Roles

Defining the brand image
Conceptualizing and designing the mobile UIUX framework

The Challenge

The main challenge was that the app was a blank slate when I first joined the team, so I had to set the foundations of the UI/UX from scratch. It had to be flexible enough to cover services ranging from a social media platform to a Yelp-like database of pet services to a pet sitter finder tool.

The Process - Logo

I designed several iterations of our logo with different font pairings. At first I focused on a dog and cat hybrid that was aimed to be a more inclusive representation of people's pets. After gathering feedback from 100 target users, the mouthless dog with the blue color was chosen as the official logo of Spetcial.

The Process - Landing Page and First Time User Experience

I researched how other websites made their landing pages conduvice to content discovery and user signup. From there, I began by sketching out a few wireframes for the first time user experience and landing page. After receiving feedback from my team as well as some of my friends, I began to flesh out the design in Illustrator.

The Process - Profile

A major requirement for the profile page was that it had to compactly include a multitude of information without overcrowding the screen. Therefore, I had to design various iterations of the profile UI based off of user testing.

Reflections

Conceptualizing this product from scratch was an enjoyable yet challenging process for me. My only regret was that I could not stray far from conventional UIs to test out new ideas due to limited time and resources.
